Text and visual specifications are the same entity

Remember how you had to manage text specifications and visual specifications separately? Those days are over. Now, you can connect a design to a description (or the other way around) with just one click.

Specify directly on mockups

You can also create specifications while working on a design. In just two clicks, simple interface will allow creating a new specification anywhere on the mockup.

  • Timeline

Every change is logged

Specify is like having your very own time machine. All the changes you make are logged, giving you the ability to go back in time and see previous alterations.

Even the smallest things

We created a changelog for each entry you post to your specification. You can see what was added, who added it, and when it was added. Impressive, huh?

  • Comments
  • Sharing

Discuss the details on the go

We created a built-in comments functionality that allows you to comment on any entry and directly create collaborative teams in the application.

Share your specifications

Just like you, we work with projects, so we understand that creating good specifications is a team effort. To help in that effort, we created a sharing feature with different sharing permissions for every project.
